VS Code shortcuts

  1. Command Palette

    Windows: CTRL+SHIFT+P

    Mac: CMD+SHIFT+P

  2. Quick Open

    Windows: CTRL+P

    Mac: CMD+P

  3. Toggle Sidebar

    Windows: CTRL+B

    Mac: CMD+B

  4. Multi-Select Cursor

    Windows: CTRL+D

    Mac: CMD+D

  5. Copy Line

    Windows: SHIFT+ALT+UP or SHIFT+ALT+DOWN

    Mac: OPT+SHIFT+UP or OPT+SHIFT+DOWN

  6. Wrap

    Windows: ALT+Z

    Mac: ALT+Z
